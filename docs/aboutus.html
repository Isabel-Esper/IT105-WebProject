About Us

We're a committed group of climbers who want to spread out love for the outdoors to all those interested in climbinf. We conduct tour guides sitable for climbers of all levels and in different settings. So it you're wanting to learn climbing techniques in an indoor gym or wanting to do some high risk outdoor bouldering- we have the people to guide you!

*Of course, each tour guide will be much more approachable if you buy them a cup of coffee beforehand.
